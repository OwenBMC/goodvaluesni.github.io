The Basics

This food co-op runs on a very simple model - we are basically a buying group. We agree on an order, then go direct to suppliers with our payment to secure our order. The entire order gets delivered at once, and you come and pick it up.

At the moment, we work with two ethical suppliers; BOXA, who provide local, organic, free range meat and Infinity Foods, who supply pretty much everything else. Read more about what these guys are up to in our FAQ.

To get involved, sign up here and we will email you about once a month letting you know when we're putting a list together, and what the suppliers have on offer. We will have an order deadline. You tell us what you want before the deadline and pay for the goods (you can write a cheque straight to the supplier if you wish). Once a month, all the products will arrive at Farset Labs. Come down and grab your orders, and that's that.

The suppliers have a huge range of food they can offer. We show you a sample of what we think is going to be most popular, but there's plenty more things "off list". The main problem is that we're working from a paper catalogue, which can cause logistical issues. If you're interested in a specific product, let us know and we'll check it out for you, or even let you browse the catalogue on the promise that we get it back!
