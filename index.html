Good Values

A food co-operative is a community-based, not for profit group that aims to provide access to ethical, good quality food at a reduced price.

We harness the power of bulk buying to get cheaper food. Where possible, Good Values food co-operative aims to buy local, organic, free range or fair trade. Most importantly, it's usually cheaper than supermarket prices

Good Values is pretty much an entirely voluntary entity - we don't make any money from the food co-operative. The food co-operative exists for the good of the community. We are currently operating in and around Belfast.
